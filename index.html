General


CozyDupe is a custom-coded LifeSteal Dupe server.
It was created by CreaCheetah and Garbos in January 2023.
It features a professionally built spawn,
a beautiful warzone (with build disabled), and a 10kx10k survival world.
Our server operates on a seasonal system!
At the end of each season, the top players and top teams are rewarded payouts.
The top players are ranked by kills and the top teams are ranked by KOTH points.
KOTH is a capturable point in the warzone, that gives the team leader 1 point every 5 minutes. Players can also create teams and play together.

Useful Commands:

  •  > /rtp       [This teleports you to a random location.]
  •  > /afk        [This teleports you to the afk area.]
  •  > /warp Crates    [This teleports you to the crates area.]
  •  > /warp KOTH   [This teleports you near KOTH.]
  •  > /team      [This lets you create/manage/view a team.]

LifeSteal
Our server is a lifesteal server!
This means that you gain 1 heart per kill and lost 1 heart per death.
Upon losing all of your hearts, you get deathbanned for 6 hours and regain 10 hearts.
You can gain hearts in different ways:

  •  > Killing other players.
  •  > Selling items to others using our auctionhouse.
  •  > Doing quests.

You can have up to 100 hearts.
Each heart you gain after reaching 100 hearts gets deposited to your heartbank!
Your heartbank stores your extra hearts and provides a damage boosts that increases the more hearts you get.

Useful commands:

  •  > /auctionhouse     [This allows you to buy items from others.]
  •  > /heartbank      [This is where your extra hearts go.]
  •  > /withdraw <number> [This is used to get hearts from your bank.]
  •  > /deposit <number>   [This is used to put hearts into your bank.]

Dupe
Our server is a dupe server!
This means that you can dupe any item except for certain special ones.
You can buy some undupable items from the shops.
You gain 1 playtime point each minute to spend in the playtime shop.
To get KOTH points, you must capture KOTH.
Shops: Heart Shop, Playtime Shop, KOTH Shop

Useful Commands:

  •  > /dupe  [This dupes your held item.]
  •  > /shop  [This opens the shop selector.]

Miscellaneous
You can find a number of unique features on our server!

  •  > Artifacts grant the user special buffs and can be obtained by killing Selene. You can only use one at a time. They are very rare.
  •  > Selene is a boss that spawns every 6 hours in the warzone. She drops an artifact box. It is not recommended fighting her unless you have 50+ hearts.
  •  > Bounties can be placed using the command and obtained by killing a player with a bounty.
  •  > Quests can be found around spawn/warzone. They can be completed once every few days and reward players with keys and hearts.
  •  > PVP Items spice up PVP. They can be seen and obtained from the item crate at spawn.


Useful Commands:

  •  > /artifact            [This allows you to select an artifact.]
  •  > /bounty<player> <number>  [This places a bounty on a player.]
  •  > /bounties          [This shows you all bounties]
